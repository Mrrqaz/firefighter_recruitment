Firefighter CV Questionnaire

Creating your tailored resume for firefighting positions

Collectively, your resume, cover letter and LinkedIn profiles all communicate your brand – who you are and what value you will bring to an organization. The following questions help us get a better understanding of your competencies, background and strengths so that we can create a resume and cover letter that reflects your individuality and brand.

Please Note:

We may not list all of your jobs on your resume; in some instances, we may:

  • Utilize a 'Previous Employment' section
  • Eliminate non-relevant or dated positions
  • Combine identical jobs at multiple locations into one listing, but separately note company names and dates of employment

You will have been asked to provide a copy of your current resume and cover letter. If you do not have a current resume, you will be able to provide information on this questionnaire. If you do not have a current cover letter, no worries! We will create one for you based on your questionnaire responses.

Please do not forward screenshots of awards, certificates, licenses or other information as we do not need to see proof; simply listing the information on your resume or in this questionnaire is sufficient.
